We strive to justify the trust our clients place in us by a professional approach, compete service and speed, as we have done for the past 30 years.

The Grafomarketing printing company was established in 1989. The initial focus was directed towards seeking out the best design solutions and development of final products according to the desires and requests of our clients. Over the course of the years, the printing company directed available resources towards the purchase of new equipment, development of our business premises and expansion of our product range.

With the acquisition of our first Xerox digital printing press in Novi Sad in 2004, we stepped into the world of digital printing.

Today, Grafomarketing is a graphics service where you can receive all kinds of services and printed products in one place. A complete business process (idea, design, graphic preparation, printing, finishing, transport and installation) in addition to a wide spectrum of printing techniques enables You to turn Your ideas into high quality final products.

With the acquisition of the second digital printing press, we became the leader in the digital printing market. It is our pleasure to use the newest generation Xerox 1000 printing press which produces superior quality at great speeds and holds the leading position in the territory of Vojvodina.

The Grafomarketing printing company conducts business with the aim to meet all client requests and expectations. Our work is performed according to the highest international standards which help us to achieve the high quality of our products and services, as well as competitive prices
