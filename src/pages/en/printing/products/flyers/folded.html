Flyers printed in standard and non-standard formats can be made with one, two, three, for or more folds. Flyers can be roll folded or Z folded. This way, You can create an interesting flyer design with a unique solution to place Your information.

  • Dimensions - upon request
  • Trim area - during prepress, the trim should be 2 mm on all sides (this surplus will be discarded after trimming)
  • Content - in the prepress files, all content should be withdrawn from the edges by 5-7 mm. 
  • RResolution - must be minimum 300 dpi (dots per inch). For any resolution below that, we do not guarantee maximum quality during printing.
  • Regimes and colour models - preparation is performed in the CMYK regime, or Grayscale if the preparation is single-colour.
  • Text - requires conversion into curves
  • Prepress - it is recommendable to send the prepress in PDF format. Attention should be paid to the page order during assembly.
  • Contact our graphic operators for all additional information
