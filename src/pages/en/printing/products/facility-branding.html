One way vision foils

One way vision foil is perforated, self-adhesive PVC foil which prevents the visibility of interior space from the printed side while the external space can be seen through the foil from the inside. To maintain this effect during night time, the external side of the foil where the print is must have a light source directed at it. Otherwise, the internal space becomes visible from the outside. Apart from shop windows, one way vision foil found its application with decorating complete glass facades and vehicle branding.

Photos Sanded foils

The fundamental and the best feature of these foils is that they can completely or partially limit the degree of visibility through the glass (offices, warehouses, laboratories) while they also enable passage of light.
They are also extremely efficient in decreasing sunlight. Sanded foils give windows the sophisticated appearance of sanded or obscure glass which can additionally be embellished by cutting out decorative drawings, signs and geometric shapes. Sanded foil can be printed, combined with self-adhesive foils in different colours or with printed stickers.

Self-adhesive glass foils

Self-adhesive glass foils or PVC foils can be matte-glossy, transparent or in colour. They can be used to completely cover windows or they can be digitally printed and the letters and shapes can be cut out on the cutter machine.

Static foils

As opposed to self-adhesive foil, static foil does not have any glue on it and is “held” onto the store windows by static electricity. Static foil can be printed and cut out on the cutting machine into desired shapes. It is most often used to produce decorative stickers or special discounts. After application, the stickers can be returned onto their protective surface, repositioned and reused. Static foil can be used on glass as well as on other smooth surfaces.

Glass stickers

Glass stickers are most frequently applied for decoration of windows and store windows, but they can also be used commercially to make products or special discounts more prominent. They can be produced by cutting coloured foil or by cutting printed self-adhesive foil on the optical eye cutter.

Sol stop foils

Sol stop foils prevent a certain spectre of sunlight from getting through the glass and additionally heating up the space and negatively affecting the exhibited products . The application of sol stop foil does not affect the visibility.
