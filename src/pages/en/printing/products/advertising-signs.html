Forex signs

Forex signs are produced by pasting printed PVC foil onto a forex board base. The entire forex surface can be covered or the foil can be cut out into specific forms which are then pasted onto the surface. Also, highly durable and abrasion resistant digital UV printing can be applied directly.

Plexiglass signs

Plexiglass signs are produced by pasting printed PVC foil onto a plexiglass sheet base. The entire plexiglass surface can be covered or the foil can be cut out into specific forms which are then pasted onto the surface. Also, highly durable and abrasion resistant digital UV printing can be applied directly.

Lexan signs

The signs are produced on a lexan base. Lexan is a light material with a honeycomb structure. It is produced in transparent and milky white. Printing is done on PVC foil. The entire lexan surface can be covered or the foil can be cut out into specific forms which are then pasted onto the surface. Also, highly durable and abrasion resistant digital UV printing can be applied directly.

Cardboard foam signse

The signs are produced on a cardboard foam base. Cardboard foam is an exceptionally light material sensitive to humidity/moisture, limiting its use to interior decoration. Printing is done on PVC foil, and then the signs are mounted onto the wall by using banner tape or silicone.

Aluminium signs

Aluminium signs are produced on an aluminium base. Usually, the entire surface of the board is pasted with printed PVC foil.

Metal signs

Metal signs are produced from metal framing with a display made of galvanized sheet metal. There is also the combination of metal framing and tarpaulin canvas. Metal signs are the best solution for road signs, construction signs and street lamp signs.

Aluminium click clack frames

Click clack frames are produced from high quality silver anodized aluminium. They consist of a back panel where the poster is mounted and a transparent protective panel which is set over the poster (it protects the poster from moisture, dust and other external influences). Replacement of the poster is performed by simply opening the removable coving strips and removing the protective panel. Aluminium frames are sold together with anti-reflex foil, a packet of dowels and screws.

A boards or sandwich boards

A boards are made of eloxated aluminium which opens and closes by click clack mechanism. The printing is protected by anti-reflex foil which prolongs its lifespan in the sense of UV protection and water-resistance. They are characterized by exceptional stability, easy replacement of printing which is generally done on poster paper, mobility, easy mounting-dismounting

Engravable plastic signs

Engravable plastic signs are used as business premises signs, front doors, office spaces, hotels, hospitality businesses, legal offices, doctor’s surgeries and others. The material used for the production of signs is engravable plastic sheets or engraving foil which is UV stable and abrasion resistant. Its standard thickness is 1.5 mm.The sheets are personalised by laser engraving which eliminates any chance of the printed letters losing or changing colour.
