Folders

  • Description
  • Prepress production

Folders have become an important detail in daily business communication. By choosing modern design, different combinations of colour, dimensions and forms, there are many opportunities to send a creative message to Your clients. Folders can be standard (with two or three clamps) or specialized with certain details (handles, pockets for CDs, business cards and brochures) which complete the visual message for the final consumers. To make Your folder look unique, You can also decide for partial UV coating, gold foil print or production of new tools.

  • Dimensions - upon request
  • Trim area - during prepress, the trim should be 5 mm on all sides (this surplus will be thrown away after trimming).
  • Content - in the prepress files, all content should be withdrawn from the edges by 5-7 mm.
  • Resolution - must be minimum 300 dpi (dots per inch). For any resolution below that, we do not guarantee maximum quality during printing.
  • Regimes and colour models - preparation is performed in the CMYK regime, or Grayscale if the preparation is single-colour.
  • Text - requires conversion into curves
  • Prepress - it is recommendable to send the prepress in PDF format. The prepress file should contain two layers. The first layer represents the appearance of the open folder for printing (PRINT), and the other layer represents the folder grid with cutting and folding lines (CUT)
  • Tools – developing new or using existing tools.
  • Contact our graphic operators for all additional information.
