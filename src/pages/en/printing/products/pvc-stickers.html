PVC stickers

  • Description
  • Prepress production

In addition to printing paper stickers, we also print stickers on PVC foil. PVC stickers have several advantages, and one of the most important ones is the possibility of sticking onto any surface, they are durable and easy to maintain. Self-adhesive PVC stickers are waterproof, resistant to solar radiation and resistant to mechanical stresses, to an extent. Stickers can be printed in various dimensions which are adjusted to the products or client requests

  • Dimensions - upon request
  • Trim area - during prepress, the trim should be 10 mm on all sides (this surplus will be discarded after trimming)
  • Content - in the prepress files, all content should be withdrawn from the edges by 10 mm.
  •  Resolution - must be 150 - 300 dpi (dots per inch). For any resolution below that, we do not guarantee maximum quality during printing.
  • Regimes and colour models - preparation is performed in the CMYK regime, or Grayscale if the preparation is single-colour
  • Text - requires conversion into curves
  • Prepress - it is recommendable to send the prepress in PDF format.
  • Foil - Specify the type of foil (matte or glossy)
