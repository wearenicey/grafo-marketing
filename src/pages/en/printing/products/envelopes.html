Envelopes

  • Description
  • Prepress production

Envelopes are graphic paper products which serve as packaging for Your parcels.
n addition to protecting and concealing information and the content of Your parcel, envelopes are also a great printed advertising product - material that contributes to visual communication and present Your company or brand.
Envelopes are printed in all sizes (American, C5, B5, C4, B4, E4, A5+, B5+, A4+, B4+ , CD envelopes) with a window on the left or right, or without a window. The papers used are from 70 g up to 100 g depending on the client’s request.

  • Resolution - must be minimum 300dpi (dots per inch). For any resolution below that, we do not guarantee maximum quality during printing.
  • Regimes and colour models - preparation is performed in the CMYK regime, or Grayscale if the preparation is single-colour.
  • Text - requires conversion into curves e
  • Prepress - it is recommendable to send the prepress in PDF format.
