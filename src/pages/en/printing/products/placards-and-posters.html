Placards and posters

  • Description
  • Prepress production

Placards and posters are promotional materials most frequently used in promotion of products and services. We print all kinds of placards and posters on a range of printing media. Printing is performed on a large quantity of material (paper, poster paper, blue back paper, photo paper) in various formats in full colour printing.

  • Dimensions - upon request
  • Trim area - during prepress, the trim should be 2 mm on all sides (this surplus will be discarded after trimming)
  • Content - during prepress, the content should be withdrawn from the edges by 5-7 mm.
  • Resolution - must be minimum 300 dpi (dots per inch). For any resolution below that, we do not guarantee maximum quality during printing.
  • Regimes and colour models - preparation is performed in the CMYK regime, or Grayscale if the preparation is single-colour.
  • Text - requires conversion into curves
  • Prepress - it is recommendable to send the prepress in PDF format.
