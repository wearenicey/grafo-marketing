Car graphics or vehicle branding is an effective mobile advertisement which is ongoing 365 days a year, and it can continue for years
Car graphics can be applied on any passenger or delivery vehicle, and is performed by pasting factory made foil, toned or previously printed foil. Car graphics can be partial which represents the printing of individual vehicle parts or complete which includes application of the desired foil onto the entire vehicle.
Foils are specially designed for vehicles and are resistant to external influences. They are characterised by longevity and stability on curved surfaces and dents. The printing cost and longevity of car graphics depend on the quality of the foil.
