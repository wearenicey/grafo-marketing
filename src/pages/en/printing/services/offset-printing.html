Offset printing

We are proud of our experienced team and the top quality offset printing they do. The Heidelberg (speedmaster and printmaster) presses are working at full capacity to produce the highest quality prints on a broad range of products, in a variety of colours, paper thickness and textures. Electronic process monitoring combined with a personal approach provides the highest level of quality in the offset printing industry.

Advantages of offset printing:

  • Printing of large and medium quantities

  • The larger the quantity - lower the individual print cost

  • Perfect precision and colour layer printing

  • Print format up to B1 (700x1000 mm)

  • Full colour reproduction

  • Saturated and high image quality

  • High spectrum of printable surfaces and colours

  • Fast and easy development of plate cylinders - longer plate lifespan
