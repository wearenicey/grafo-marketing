UV printing

View Photos

UV printing enables fast and direct printing in full colour with the possibility of printing the colour white onto almost all kinds of material.

This technique significantly accelerates the printing preparation process, as the preparation is greatly shorter when compared to other techniques. The printable materials are glass, metal, wood, plastic, ceramics, textile, paper, carton, plexiglass, forex, aluminium, juvidur, melamine particle boards, mirrors, kitchen cabinets, self-adhesive foil, eco-leather, metallic foil, magnets, canvas, transparent materials, etc

Advantages of UV printing:

  • High print quality

  • Speed

  • High precision

  • Possibility of variable data printing for each individual unit

  • Small, medium and large quantities

  • Good ratio of price and quality
