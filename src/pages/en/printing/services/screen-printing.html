Screen printing

Screen printing is one of the oldest printing techniques and is therefore not competitive to other printing procedures, but rather complements them with its special abilities.

. This printing technique is very popular for printing large quantities as the individual print cost in large series is considerably smaller than for other printing processes. It is suitable for printing on ceramics, wood, plastic, metal and glass surfaces, while its widest application can be found in the textile industry, for printing on various textile materials.
