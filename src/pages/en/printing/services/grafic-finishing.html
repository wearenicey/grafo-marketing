Graphic finishing

Our bookbindery performs the finishing on all of our products.

The finishing procedures after printing include binding of books, brochures, magazines and catalogues by using different technological procedures (hardback and paperback, spiral binding). Cutting, creasing, binding, final binding. Stacking the printed sheet into books, brochures, magazines, catalogues and others, which are performed by folding, assembling, stitching, joining, gluing and sewing. Prints made on paper and carton such as business forms, labels, calendars, promotional materials, brochures and others, which are performed by folding, gluing, piercing, perforating, relief modelling, coating with plastic and others.
