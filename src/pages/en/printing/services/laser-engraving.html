Laser engraving

Laser engraving is the process of using a laser beam to burn, melt and vaporise the materials which are exposed to the laser beam.

There are many advantages of laser engraving: Ti is contactless, the process is clean and dry, Laser engraving can be performed on metals and non-metals (wood, plexiglass, leather, cardboard, rubber, cork, plastic)

Advantages of using laser:

  • Suitable for various materials

  • Possibility of creating irregular shape forms

  • High precision

  • Speed

  • Possibility of producing trial samples

  • Production of products in small, medium and large quantities
