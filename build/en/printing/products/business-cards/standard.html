Business cards speak about Your brand and enable You to impress Your clients and maintain contact with them. You only have one opportunity to leave a good first impression on Your potential clients. Choose a unique design and the best quality to promote the image of Your company.

Share

  • Dimensions - in the format 90 mm x 50 mm or according to the European standard 85 mm x 55 mm
  • Trim area - should be 92 mm x 52 mm or 87 mm x 57 mm
  • Content - in the prepress files, all content should be withdrawn from the edges of the final business card format by 3-5 mmÂ 
  • Resolution - must be minimum 300 dpi (dots per inch). For any resolution below that, we do not guarantee maximum quality during printing.
  • Regimes and colour models - preparation is performed in the CMYK regime, or Grayscale if the preparation is single-colour
  • Text - requires conversion into curves
  • Prepress - it is recommendable to send the prepress in PDF format
  • Contact our graphic operators for all additional information.

  • Plastification is used to give a certain product a luxurious and more expensive look, to protect it or to reinforce it. Plastification can be matte or glossy, one-sided or two-sided (viewed as one sheet of paper).
  • Cornering is the process of rounding the corners, it is performed on two, three or all four corners.
  • Varnishing is used if the product has large areas covered in dark colours, making fingerprints visible after use, or when the product cannot sustain the cost of plastification or when plastification of the product is inappropriate. As plastification, varnishing can be matte or glossy, one-sided or two-sided (compared to one sheet of paper).
  • Clear varnish supports quality by increasing print saturation, making colours more rich and realistic. Apart from that, it balances halftones and provides a clearer experience of details and the 3D effect. By selectively applying the Clear Dry toner, You can highlight and focus attention to the sections You want.
